Common Adulterants Found in Milk

Protect your family from these harmful substances commonly found in adulterated milk

Urea

Used to increase protein content artificially, causing kidney damage and serious health complications.

Water

Added to increase quantity, but it dilutes essential nutrients and vitamins.

Detergent/Shampoo

Contains surfactants like sodium lauryl sulfate, which create bubbles and give milk a frothy appearance.

Starch

Added to increase thickness and make milk appear richer, but reduces nutritional value.

Formalin

Used as a preservative but is highly toxic and can cause cancer and other serious diseases.

Hydrogen Peroxide

Used to increase shelf life but can cause gastric irritation and damage to internal organs.
